CTU Tech Fest 2024

Get ready for CTU Tech Fest 2024, the premier event of the year for all tech enthusiasts at City Technical University! Join us in September for an action-packed week of innovation, creativity, and learning. This year's fest features hands-on workshops, insightful talks from industry leaders, and exciting competitions that will challenge your skills and ignite your passion for technology. Whether you're a coding wizard, a design guru, or simply curious about the future of tech, there's something for everyone. Don't miss out on this opportunity to connect, collaborate, and celebrate the best in technology at CTU!
